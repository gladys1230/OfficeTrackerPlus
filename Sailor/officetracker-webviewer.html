Office Tracker WebViewer Share "View Only" Schedules from Web Browsers


Share schedules within your office or around the world. Office Tracker WebViewer lets you see schedules right in most web browsers.

The Office Tracker WebViewer works with the Office Tracker scheduling system to provide "view only" access to schedule information to anyone with a web browser. You can allow access to schedule information to anyone on the internet by giving them the appropriate URL and password information.

This is great for organizations that want to share community event information, class schedules, facility availability, equipment sharing, public events and similar time-based information. The affordability of the WebViewer makes it ideal when you need a large number of people to be able to see schedules but you do not want everyone making changes.

If you need full scheduling access via the web, you may want to learn more about the Office Tracker Web-based Client. In addition to full viewing capabilities the Web-based Client lets users schedule and edit schedules right in your web browser. You can choose what works best for your needs- view access only with the Office Tracker WebViewer or full scheduling access with the Office Tracker 7 Web-based Client.

There are many ways the WebViewer can be customized to provide additional functionality. For example, you can automatically post upcoming events on your own web site.
