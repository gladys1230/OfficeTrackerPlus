Grgich Hills Expands Wine Tours/Events with Office Tracker Scheduling


We test drove about five different systems before we decided that Office Tracker met our current and future needs
― Violet Grgich, V.P. Operations Grgich Hills

Founded in 1977, Grgich Hills is an organic and Biodynamic certified winery in the Napa Valley region of California. Grgich wines uses only grapes that have been farmed Bio dynamically an agricultural philosophy that treats the Earth as a living being and utilizes natures basic life forces, natural componse and nutient preparations, and cosmic cycles to bring about balance between the grapevies and the Earth. Completely estate grown, Grgich Hills controls every step of the wine making process, from vineyards, to the finished bottle of wine.

Bob During, Wine Clue Coordinator gave us some background: "Prior to Office Tracker we were doing all of our Tasting Room tour and event scheduling by hand in a yearly calendar. Because we are always trying to improve on what we do and to offer more to our customers we felt we needed a better scheduling system."

Bob continued, "Although the time it took using the previous method to enter the original tour, tasting or event was not as efficient as we wanted. The place where we see the biggest difference is that we can now schedule multiple events in different locations, at the same time and the ways with which we can move a scheduled ooking from one date and time to a different date. No more erasing and re-writting, forgetting credit card or re-entering phone numbers. All the detailed information for each visitor transfers with ease. In addition, many people have access to the schedule from different locations, which makes our response time to our customers faster and more accureate."

There are three people who use Office Tracker on a daily basis and two who use in one occasion. They use Office Tracker to schedule tastins, tours, and special events at a winery. Office Tracker allows them to quickly see when time would be available for a tour, tasting or special event. They print the schedule out each morning to assign a staff member to each group for visitor.

"Office Tracker filled our present need by giving us the flexibility to expand our current offerings while at the same time providing us with the ability to increase the number of events we can offer as we continue to enhance the winery experience at Grgich Hills. We are very happy with Office Tracker and how it's working for us!" said Bob.

Tomball Regional Medical Center Replaces Dry Erase Board with Office Tracker Scheduling


We know we are saving at least one hour a day in moving from a dry erase board to the PC based software. This alone would add up to several hundred dollars in a single month.

Tomball Regional Medical Center is a 350 bed Acute Care Hospital. Before Office Tracker scheduling for the Imaging and Rehab staff was done on a dry erase marker board or printed paper schedules out and posted them on a wall. The schedules were not remotely accessible so users had to physically come to view them throughout the day, it was also difficult to change, so mistakes were more likely to occur. With dry erase boards a great deal of information had to be written over again just to move a single time slot which was clumsy and time consuming. In addition the big board was not visually pleasing and did not fully represent the workload for the day.

Phil Regas, Director of CV Imaging/Cardia Rehab had used the Office Tracker software at two other facilities. The decision to implement it at the Tomball Regional Medical Center was made in cooperation with a small group. The Departmental Director championed it and the staff liked it immediately.

Office Tracker provides immediate access to the schedules for all involved staff members, it also provides remote viewing, and the use of timed email alerts to remind physicians of upcoming appointments. Regas, said "With Office Tracker we now use 3 large screen LCD's and have 6 remote PC's that are able to access and make changes to the schedule. The Director can look at the schedule from his office and by using status color changes he can get an immediate understanding of where the work is in relation to the day.

We know we are saving at least one hour per day in moving from a dry erase board to the PC based software. This alone would add up to several hundred dollars in a single month. In addition, we have reduced errors such as writing out the wrong procedure. We also save in paper to print schedules out for other departments, and we save the time it used to take in transporting the paper schedules to each department or individual. We have improved our customer satisfaction by using the new system to update patient families.

DATV is the non-profit, community television station in Dayton, OH. Citizens who use our video editing facilities have always had to sign up using the paper calendar method. After they find an available room and date, they would then fill out a request form, give it to a staff member who walked it back to the calendar and manually wrote in their request. Additionally, we have citizens calling and asking if a specific date, room, and time are available. Our staff would then have to get up, walk back to the room where the calendars are located (but a phone isn’t) just to see if that that date, room and time was available. As you can imagine, if it was not available, you could be doing a lot of walking and talking back and forth. In addition, at the end of each year we would manually count up the hours used on each of 6 to 8 calendars for use in our Annual Reports.

We needed a program that could be easily learned and easily used. We also needed a calendar that could be posted to the web and viewed by community members from the comfort of their own homes. Office Tracker fit our needs perfectly. As one who hates to read documentation, the demo was so intuitive; I just started playing around Office Tracker and had it doing everything we needed it to do. Not only does it [Office Tracker] save DATV money, but as importantly it saves us time. Concurrently, the time, effort and money it saves our members not having to drive into our facility to sign up for an Edit Suite or Studio is immeasurable. As a community based organization, it’s our mission to serve the citizens of Dayton in the most effective way possible. Office Tracker is one of the best tools for helping us meet that mission.

All 10 employees schedule members for editing and studio usage. Often, we’re busy assisting other members with technical needs. As a result, we have to stop helping them in order to sign someone up for a room. Now not only can people call in their request, they can email their request. This obviously frees up staff to take care of more pressing issues.

The key benefits are: simple; ease of use for staff, easy to understand calendars, powerful interface, and comprehensive and easy to understand reports. Office Tracker is exactly what we were looking for. Not only does it save a tremendous amount of time in signing people up. It saves us a great deal of time producing our year-end statistics. No more manually entering or manually counting the hours used. Steve Ross

When we found and started using Office Tracker, it was clear that this scheduling program would allow us to efficiently incorporate the personnel and equipment to meet our needs. We have used Office Tracker ever since. We now spend maybe 20 minutes of administrative time on scheduling. Office Tracker is saving us about 15% in administrative costs just in the reduction of time needed to schedule projects. We estimate another 15% in savings costs for easier communication between the office and the crews

Office Tracker is now not just used for scheduling job personnel and equipment. We also use Office Tracker to schedule sales appointments, vacation days, and equipment maintenance.

The Key Benefit of Office Tracker is the ability to efficiently manage and coordinate our equipment and personnel resources. Office Tracker's integrated scheduling improved our efficiency and communication.
