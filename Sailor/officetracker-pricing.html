Pricing Information

Office Tracker is licensed based on the number of users(devices) accessing the system. If you will be scheduling and tracking resources such as rooms, facilities, equipment and similar items, you do not need to add these into your total users. Monthly hosted options are available as are longer term licenses.

Office Tracker offers discounted pricing for Educational, Government and Non-Profit Organizations.

Upgrades

Office Tracker upgrades are usually available for a special discount to registered users of previous versions, please be sure to contact us for current pricing information if you already own a license of Office Tracker.

Adding Users

Once you purchase a license for a certain number of users you very likely will want to expand at a later time - adding more users to the system. Office Tracker allows you to add users as you grow, just contact us for a free quote.
