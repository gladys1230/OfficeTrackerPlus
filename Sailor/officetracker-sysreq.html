Office Tracker System Requirements

System requirements can change over time due to changes made in the operating system software purchased with your machine as well as updates you do to the machine and the way you choose to use the software. Following are the minimum requirements for Office Tracker verion 12.

Office Tracker Server Requirements

Note: Laptop computers (notebooks, etc.) are not supported for server use
Processor: 2.0 GHz or better*
Operating System: Windows 7/ Windows 8/ Windows 10 / Windows 2008 and R2/ Windows 2012 and R2/ Windows 2016/Linux**
Network: TCP/IP Network Support RAM: 4GB RAM*
Hard Drive: 8 GB free disk space*
Web Browser: Internet Explorer vs. 10 or later, Firefox 10 or later - Javascript required

Other browsers and other versions of browsers may work but have not been tested and may not be supported. Any browser used must support CSS2 or later and JavaScript 1.2 or later.

Office Tracker Client System Requirements

Processor: 1 GHz or greater
Operating System: Windows 7/ Windows 8/ Windows 10 / Windows 2008/ Windows 2012/ Windows 2016
RAM: 1 GB RAM* Network: TCP/IP Network Support
Hard Drive: 1 GB free disk space*
Monitor: VGA or SVGA video monitor and 256 colors
Resolution: 1024x768 or larger screen area recommended

Office Tracker Web-based Client and Web Viewer System Requirements

Software: Internet Explorer vs. 10 or later, Firefox 10 or later- JavaScript required
RAM: 1 GB RAM* Network: TCP/IP Network Support
Monitor: VGA or SVGA video monitor and 256 colors
Resolution: 1024x768 or larger screen area recommended

Note: Other browsers and versions of browsers may work but have not been tested and may not be supported. Any browser used must support CSS2 or later and JavaScript 1.2 or later.

*Memory, processor and disk space requirements may be considerably higher than the above defaults, depending on the number of users and the number of events you schedule

**Office Tracker may not run and is not supported with Operating Systems not on this list. Contact Milum Corporation for details.

**Linux Users
Please contact Milum for details on how to install/use Office Tracker on the Linux Operating System.
